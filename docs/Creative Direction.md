# RAZEVÉL — Creative Direction

## Design Philosophy

RAZEVÉL's visual language is derived from the intersection of Indian craft heritage and European quiet luxury. We take equal inspiration from:

- The restraint of Hermès
- The editorial tension of Maison Margiela
- The heritage depth of Sabyasachi
- The digital sophistication of Apple

But we build something that is entirely our own — a language that will, over time, become recognizable as distinctly **RAZEVÉL**.

---

## Visual Principles

### 1. Negative Space is Sacred
Every section breathes. We never fill space for the sake of filling it. A large empty area of ivory or obsidian communicates confidence that cheap brands cannot afford.

### 2. Typography is Architecture
Headlines are structural. They hold the page together. Use Playfair Display as load-bearing columns — heavy in feeling, light in weight.

### 3. Gold is Earned
Gold (#D4AF37) appears as an accent — never a background. It marks the points of highest importance. Used sparingly, it retains its power.

### 4. Images Tell, Not Sell
Photography never shows products on hangers or in a shop. Every image is editorial — models in environments, hands at work, fabric in motion.

### 5. Motion is Invisible
Animations do not announce themselves. They make content feel more alive, not more flashy. Slow reveals. Subtle parallax. Elegant hovers.

## Layout System

### Grid
- 12-column grid with generous gutters (clamp 1.5rem → 4rem)
- Maximum container width: 88rem
- Sections alternate between obsidian and ivory backgrounds
- Asymmetric layouts preferred over symmetrical grids

### Spacing Rhythm
- Section padding: clamp(5rem, 10vw, 10rem)
- Component gap: clamp(3rem, 6vw, 6rem)
- Content gap: clamp(1.5rem, 3vw, 3rem)

### Editorial Numbers
Large translucent section numbers (01, 02, 03...) positioned as typographic decoration behind content — creates depth and editorial character.

## Component Aesthetic

### Buttons
- Primary: Gold fill, obsidian text — used for the single most important CTA per section
- Secondary: Gold border, gold text with hover fill — for secondary actions
- Ghost: Bottom border only, text changes to gold on hover

### Cards
Never "product cards." Every piece of content is presented as an editorial fragment — cropped photography, large typography, intentional whitespace.

### Navigation
- Transparent on entry
- Frosted glass (backdrop-filter) after 60px scroll
- Gold 1px bottom border on scroll
- Logo always centered
- Links at 0.65rem, letter-spacing 0.2em, all caps
