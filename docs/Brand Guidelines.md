# RAZEVÉL — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Name:** RAZEVÉL  
**Tagline:** Crafted for Forever.  
**Est.:** 2026  
**Origin:** Mumbai, India  
**Industry:** Luxury Indian Ethnic Wear & Couture

---

## Brand Positioning

RAZEVÉL occupies the highest tier of Indian ethnic luxury — above premium, below museum-grade art. We are not aspirational. We are understated. Our clients do not need to show off their wealth. They need a garment that will outlive them.

## Typography

### Primary: Playfair Display
- Used for: Headlines, display text, brand name, italic pull quotes
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
- Use italic sparingly — only for emotive emphasis

### Secondary: Montserrat
- Used for: Body copy, eyebrows, labels, captions, CTAs, navigation
- Weights: 200 (ExtraLight), 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold)
- All caps with high letter-spacing (0.2–0.3em) for labels

### Typography Scale
| Name | Size | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Display XL | clamp(3rem, 7vw, 6.5rem) | Hero headlines |
| Display LG | clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 5rem) | Section headlines |
| Display MD | clamp(2rem, 4vw, 3.5rem) | Sub-headlines |
| Editorial | clamp(1rem, 2vw, 1.25rem) | Lead body copy |
| Body | 0.875–1rem | Regular body |
| Caption/Label | 0.6–0.75rem | Eyebrows, labels, metadata |

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Quiet authority. Never loud. Never aggressive.

**Writing Principles:**
- Use em dashes liberally — they slow the reader.
- Sentences should breathe. Short and certain.
- Never explain luxury. Let it be felt.
- Avoid superlatives unless factual ("200–800 hours" is more powerful than "exceptionally detailed").
- First person plural ("We craft...") feels more intimate than passive voice.

**Words we use:** Craft · Heritage · Legacy · Bespoke · Handcrafted · Intention · Singular · Devotion · Atelier · Forever

**Words we never use:** Sale · Fast · Affordable · Trendy · Collection Drop · Ready-to-wear
