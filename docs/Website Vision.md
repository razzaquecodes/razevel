# RAZEVÉL — Website Vision

## The Digital Atelier

The RAZEVÉL website is not a store. It is not a portfolio. It is a **digital atelier** — a space that communicates the ethos of our brand before a single garment is seen.

When a visitor lands on razevel.com, they should feel:
- They have entered somewhere exclusive
- They should move slowly
- Every pixel has been considered
- This brand does not need their attention — it is comfortable with silence

---

## User Experience Principles

### Visitors Slow Down
The website deliberately slows the visitor. Not through frustration — through magnetism. Content reveals itself. Text unfolds. Images breathe. The visitor is drawn in, not rushed through.

### Luxury Loading
The entry experience begins before the homepage is visible. The RZ monogram draws itself with a path animation. The brand name fades in. Then the world opens.

### Smooth Scrolling
Lenis provides ultra-smooth scroll with a duration of 1.4 seconds. The easing curve is exponential — it decelerates gently, never stopping abruptly.

### Custom Cursor
A gold dot cursor with a lagging gold ring. On hover over interactive elements, the ring expands. This is the visitor's primary tactile interaction with the brand's luxury.

### Reveal Animations
All content reveals on scroll — but never abruptly. Text slides up from a hidden position. Images fade in with a subtle scale. Sections emerge, they do not appear.

## Section-by-Section Intentions

### Hero
The visitor should say: "Oh." One word. One breath. The gold lehenga against obsidian. The words "Crafted For Forever" arriving one by one. The stats at the bottom giving context. The scroll indicator beckoning.

### Brand Story
Where the brand becomes human. The father's hands. The workshop. The craft. This section should make someone who was about to leave — stay.

### Featured Collection
Not products. Pieces. Each with its own character, mood, weight. The grid feels like pages of a museum catalogue.

### Craftsmanship
The most important section. This is where we earn trust. Show the process. Show the hours. Show the artisan hands. Let the visitor understand why the price is what it is.

### Atelier
Fullscreen. Immersive. Still. The viewer is standing inside the studio. They should want to be there.

### Bespoke
The pinnacle of our offering. Clear feature list. Direct CTA. This section converts the most serious clients.

### Client Journey
Transparency. We show exactly what happens when someone chooses RAZEVÉL. No mystery (except the garment itself).

### Journal
The brand has intellectual depth. We write about craft. About tradition. About what luxury means in India. The journal positions RAZEVÉL as a cultural institution, not just a clothing brand.

## Technical Philosophy

Every technical decision reflects the brand:

| Value | Technical Expression |
|---|---|
| Slowness | Lenis smooth scroll, 1.4s duration |
| Precision | TypeScript, clean architecture |
| Elegance | CSS custom properties, no utility soup |
| Performance | Next.js App Router, image optimization |
| Luxury | Custom cursor, loading screen, reveal animations |

---

## Success Criteria

The website succeeds if:
1. A visitor spends more than 3 minutes on the homepage
2. The brand feels expensive before they read a single word
3. A high-intent visitor immediately knows how to begin their bespoke journey
4. The website would be recognized as premium by someone who has never heard of RAZEVÉL
